Who We Are

Founded in 2017, Phoebus Technology Company Limited is a leading provider of advanced oilfield solutions. Our team of experts specializes in reservoir optimization, well integrity, and production enhancement technologies. Operating out of Hong Kong, we serve clients across Asia with a focus on increasing operational efficiency and sustainability.

We are committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions, including cementing, fracturing, and enhanced oil recovery (EOR), ensuring the highest level of performance and safety in the energy industry. Our goal is to help our clients maximize their resources while minimizing environmental impact.
